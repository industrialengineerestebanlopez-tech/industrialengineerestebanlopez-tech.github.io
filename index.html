About

Engineer with a quantitative core

I am an engineer who likes to understand how a complex system performs, fails, and can be improved — and to back every conclusion with data.

My foundation is a B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering (240 ECTS). Across my degree I worked on real, hands-on engineering: FEA and CFD simulations, CAM/CNC manufacturing, structural analysis, maintenance management and a thesis quantifying the performance and reliability of an automated production line.

I am now extending that mindset into GNSS and satellite navigation through the JSNP master. The bridge is deliberate: the same rigor I used for system availability and reliability in industry maps directly onto GNSS performance — accuracy, integrity, availability and continuity.

I completed my engineering degree entirely alongside a full-time job in Germany — a fair summary of how I work: focused, reliable and persistent.
